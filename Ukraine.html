Ukraine

If the ordinary person will think about Ukraine, the first thing that will probably go on mind is Chornobyl on HBO and Ukrainian new comedien president. But here I would like to present the few place from Ukraine that are my favorite.

Kiev

Kiev remains to be my favoriote city and my "exhome" for 14 past years. In Kiev you can find some combination with old history, USSR buildings and new modern city, combined with a cheap restourants and bars.

Lviv

I would call Lviv the second visited city in Ukraine after Kiev. Unique history and style of the city will make you feel like home. Be ready to wait around two hours to get in to the most popular restourants.

Odesa

Odesa is the port city on the Black sea. Great place to spent your vocation if you are looking for great parties, culture and rest near the sea. For people who are fond of balet or opera there is Odessa Opera and Ballet Theater.

Bukovel

Bukovel is a ski resort. It is opened all year amnd everyone can find some things to do. In summer you can rent a bike, go to swim in the lake or hike in the mountains. I winter its a classical ski resort with modern liftings and opened warm swimmingpool.
